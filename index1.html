The Eureka Nevada Mystery

Eureka Nevada was settled in 1864 Eureka Nevada by silver prospectors. Today about 450 people live there. Although it's a small town, it does have an opera house and a few museums. There is no real industry in the town. Despite the only business in the town undoubtably being service based, the median income for strictly male full time workers is $85,000 a year. U.S Census Although the town has roughly 450 residents, 218 are male and and 232 are female. Despite the ratio being close to 50-50, males in Eureka who work make an average $72,000 a year; where females in the same make only an average $18,000. This may be because more women choose to be homemakers, but the numbers show that's not quite right either. fully employed females only make an average of $31,000 a year and fully employeed males make $85,000. Not only is this so incredibly unequal, but it's also a little strange that such a small town with no nearby industry is able to make $85,000 a year anyways.
